5 websites for reading ebooks online for free

This blog post lists one of the five very popular websites for reading or downloading ebooks online. The five websites in the blog are internet archive, google books, open library, wikibooks and project gutenberg.

01. Internet archive

Internet archive is an online library with millions of free books, movies, software, music, websites and more.

02. Google books

Google books can be downloaded from books.google.com or google play books store. There are ebooks of different categories such as fiction, literature, computers or technology. There are also audiobooks and comics on fiction, literature, science fiction, science, technology and general.

03. Open Library

Open library is an online library with millions of free ebooks. There are ebooks available on different subjects such as science, physics, chemistry, science fiction, short stories and textbooks.

04. Wikibooks

Wikibooks is a library of free digital textbooks. There are books on many different subjects such as science, engineering and computing.

05. Project gutenberg

Project gutenberg is an online library of more than 60, 000 free ebooks. There are many different categories of ebooks such as physics, chemistry, science, science fiction, short stories, astronomy, microscopy and microbiology.
